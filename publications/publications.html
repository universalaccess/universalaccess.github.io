Information Platforms

  • The Energy Access Portal at Energypedia

    Energypedia, in collaboration with WAME, launched the Energy Access Portal on Energypedia at the Sustainable Energy for All Forum in Lisbon, Portugal on 2-3 May, 2018. The Energy Access Portal provides an overview of the information on energypedia related to Energy Access. The Energy Access aims to be the central hub for all information related to energy access issues, consolidating articles, publications, tools and related resources on topics such as technology options for energy access, the policy framework for energy access and planning energy access projects. Along with access to information, energy professionals and enthusiasts can share their knowledge and experiences on the portal.

  • World Access to Modern Energy (WAME)

    WAME contributes to the Sustainable Development Goal 7 by sharing learning material and stories from the field, providing access to more than 1250 open source publications and case studies focusing on access to energy. The WAME Project strives to contribute to a world where lack of access to energy is no longer a hindrance to human and economic development.

    WAME aims to share knowledge on the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal number 7, raise awareness of its importance, and monitor progress toward reducing the number of people without access to reliable, sustainable and modern energy, at affordable prices. WAME focuses on increasing understanding, inspire action and reinforce a sense of hope in the future.
